I'm a french research engineer at the [XLIM](https://www.xlim.fr/en) institute (Poitiers, FRANCE) in the Computer Graphics team [ASALI-IG](https://www.xlim.fr/en/research/mathematics-computer-science-image/image-synthesis-and-analysis/ig) working on discrete geometry, lighting simulation and the modeling and animation of geometric objects.

I completed my PhD at [XLIM](https://www.xlim.fr/en) institute in Limoges (FRANCE) in the Realistic Images Synthesis team [ASALI-SIR](https://www.xlim.fr/en/research/mathematics-computer-science-image/image-synthesis-and-analysis/sir) working on procedural texture synthesis using procedural noise functions with a rendering perspective (Antialiasing, Normal Mapping, Shading, Performance) in order to devise better live-authoring tools for artists. 

I'm interested in procedural modeling, demoscene and new api/tools to play with.
