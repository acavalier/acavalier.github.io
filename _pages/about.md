I'm a french PHD student at the [XLIM](https://www.xlim.fr/en) institute (Limoges, FRANCE) in the Realistic Images Synthesis team [ASALI-SIR](https://www.xlim.fr/en/research/mathematics-computer-science-image/image-synthesis-and-analysis/sir). 

I'm currently working on procedural texture synthesis using procedural noise functions with a rendering perspective (Antialiasing, Normal Mapping, Shading, Performance) in order to devise better live-authoring tools for artists. I'm also interested in demoscene and new api/tools to play with.
